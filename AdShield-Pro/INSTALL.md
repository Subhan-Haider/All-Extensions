### For Chrome, Edge, Brave, or any Chromium-based browser:

1. **Open Extensions Page**

4. **Pin the Extension** (Optional but recommended)

   - Find "AdShield Pro" in the list

5. **Start Blocking Ads!** üéâ

## First-Time Setup

After installation:

1. **Check the Extension is Active**
   - Click the AdShield Pro icon
   - Ensure the toggle switch is ON (purple/blue)

2. **Test It Out**
   - Visit a website with ads (e.g., news sites)
   - Click the extension icon to see blocked ads count
   - Watch the statistics increase!

3. **Whitelist Trusted Sites** (Optional)
   - Visit a site you want to support
   - Click the AdShield Pro icon
   - Click "Whitelist Site"

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Not Working?

1. **Refresh the page** - After installing, reload the webpage
2. **Check if enabled** - Make sure the toggle is ON
3. **Check whitelist** - Ensure the site isn't whitelisted
4. **Reload extension** - Go to extensions page and click the refresh icon

### No Statistics Showing?

- Statistics update in real-time as you browse
- Visit websites with ads to see the counter increase
- Some sites may not have trackable ads

### Website Not Loading Properly?

- Some sites detect ad blockers
- Try whitelisting the site if you trust it
- Click "Whitelist Site" in the popup
